Vitals App Updates

Last Updated:

November 2025

Role:

Programmer

Obligation:

Modern Motion Pictures LLC

Type:

Unity Apps

MMP has an app made in Unity that has been used for multiple shows called Vitals. It is an app designed to imitate everything that a heart rate monitor machine in a hospital would do. All the settings and cues can be configured in the app itself by a user. They can set the range in which the heart rate, blood pressure, respiration, and various other values jump around between to simulate any condition a script would call for. They can also choose between different waveforms that then draw based off of their correlating value as well.

My Responsibilities

While I did not work at MMP during its initial development I have been assigned to be its caretaker in many ways since working there. This has included fixing bugs and implementing new functionality when needed such as new waveforms for different medical conditions as well as new theme layouts with new medical graphs.
