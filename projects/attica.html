Attica

Completed:

January 2017

Role:

Designer, Programmer

Obligation:

Hobbyist

Type:

Stencyl Flash Game

Attica is a lane based defense game made by myself, Austin Orlik, Benjamin Panfil, and Warren Guilles in a 48 hour game jam. Warren and I coded the game in Stencyl, Benjamin did the sound design and UI in Illustrator, while Austin did most of the art in Photoshop.

I specifically worked on implementing character movement, boat A.I., U.I., and helped work on additional programming.
